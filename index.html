I am working as a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Dept. of Electrical & Computer Engineering, Sungkyunkwan University and also affiliated with Automation Lab under the guidance of Professor Jae Wook Jeon. My research areas of interest are Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Image Processing,and Surveillance System. I am also passionate about designing vision-based solutions to challenging problems and deploying them to mobile and edge devices.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from Sungkyunkwan University in 2021; a M.Tech degree in Information Technology Management and a B.Sc. degree in Computer Science from Vietnam National University HCMC, International University. Before joining AI Research, I worked on developing computer vision system for traffic surveillance (vehicle detection & tracking, traffic flow estimation) and developing industrial mobile applications.
